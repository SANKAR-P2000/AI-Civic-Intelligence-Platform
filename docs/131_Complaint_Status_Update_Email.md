# Complaint Status Update Email

## Project

AI Civic Intelligence Platform (AICIP)

---

# Phase

Phase 17 – Notifications & Email Module

---

# Objective

Automatically notify citizens whenever the status of their complaint changes.

---

# Trigger

Complaint status updated by an ADMIN or GOVERNMENT user.

---

# Workflow

Admin / Government

↓

Update Complaint Status

↓

Complaint Saved

↓

Email Service

↓

Gmail SMTP

↓

Citizen Inbox

---

# Email Subject

Complaint Status Updated

---

# Email Content

- Citizen Name
- Complaint ID
- Updated Complaint Status

---

# Benefits

- Keeps citizens informed.
- Improves transparency.
- Demonstrates event-driven backend communication.

---

# Technologies

- Spring Boot Mail
- JavaMailSender
- Gmail SMTP
