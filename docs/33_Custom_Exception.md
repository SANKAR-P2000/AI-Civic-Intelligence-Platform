# Custom Exception

---

## Purpose

A custom exception represents a specific business error.

Instead of throwing a generic RuntimeException, we create meaningful exception classes.

---

## Current Exception

EmailAlreadyExistsException

---

## Why?

Instead of:

RuntimeException

We use:

EmailAlreadyExistsException

This makes the code:

- Easier to understand
- Easier to maintain
- Easier to handle globally

---

## Benefits

- Better readability
- Business-specific exceptions
- Professional architecture
