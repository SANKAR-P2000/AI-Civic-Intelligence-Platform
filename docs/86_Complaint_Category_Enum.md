# ComplaintCategory Enum

## Objective

Create an enumeration representing the different categories of civic complaints.

---

## Categories

- ROAD_DAMAGE
- STREET_LIGHT
- GARBAGE
- WATER_SUPPLY
- DRAINAGE
- TRAFFIC
- PUBLIC_TRANSPORT
- ENVIRONMENT
- OTHER

---

## Purpose

- Standardize complaint categories.
- Improve filtering and searching.
- Support reporting and analytics.
- Enable future AI-based classification.

---

## Benefits

- Prevents invalid category values.
- Improves maintainability.
- Ensures consistent database values.
